Bad Activist Collective is a space dedicated to connecting activists, justice seekers and change makers from all around the globe to combat systems of oppression and fight for a better future for people & the planet.

On the Bad Activist site you can learn more about the collective, read blog posts and see art from collective members and contributors, listen to the Bad Activist podcast and connect with us to contribute a piece yourself!

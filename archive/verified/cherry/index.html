Volunteering Certificate: Cherry Sagar

To Whomsoever It May Concern

Ms. Cherry Sagar has been volunteering with There Is No Earth B since April 2021. During her time here, she has contributed her time and effort in different domains and has added value to the organisation.

She joined TINEB at a time when the organisation’s work was focused on Covid relief work. Along with the team, she enabled us to reach and aid 4.84 lakh people – helping them access doctor’s consultation, medication, hospital facilities, homemade food, and more.

The purpose of this letter is to provide a recommendation for Cherry Sagar, who has been a volunteer with There Is No Earth B since April 2021. During the second wave of covid-19 pandemic in India, Cherry volunteered to identify, verify and communicate resources to the needy. On ground, she’s been volunteering regularly with us at our cleanup drives in the Forests of Delhi.

Her attention to detail and work ethic is incredible. She is talented, dependable, and hardworking. Beyond that, she is an impressive singer. Through her beautiful voice she has spread awareness about our conservation efforts around Sanjay Van, a Forest in Delhi. On the whole her volunteer efforts with and contributions to the team have had a positive impact and are greatly appreciated.

I can confidently say that Cherry Sagar did an excellent job in completing all the allotted tasks and participated enthusiastically. I have no doubts that she will surely prove to be a strong addition to your institution and shine.
